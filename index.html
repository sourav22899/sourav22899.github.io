Sourav Sahoo

I am a third-year PhD student at the MIT Operations Research Center, where I am advised by Prof. Negin Golrezaei. My research interests lie at the intersection of computer science, operations research, and economics, with a current focus on online learning and auctions.
Previously, I earned my Dual Degree (B.Tech & M.Tech) in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Madras, advised by Prof. Abhishek Sinha.
